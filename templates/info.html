General Information

Welcome to Superposition Smash!

Included here are some some simple instructions so that you can make the most of this application. If you have any quesions or issues you should first ask other users or admins.

First note that if you are using a mobile to access this web app, that you will probably find the experience better in landscape view.

When you first logged in you should have first seen your "account details" page. Here you can find all kinds of statistics on your past performance for you rating, coin, or handicap. These stats will continue to be updated as you play more tournaments.

On the right of your "account details" will be a section to change your password. It is recommended to do this as soon as possible, but make sure not to use any passwords you use for other applications as security for this password is minimal and not guaranteed. Simply enter your old and new password and click "change" to update.

You can logout by clicking "logout" on the right side of your "account details". This will ensure others can not access your account on the same device without entering the correct password.

Click "tournaments" on the right side of your "account details" page to access the list of all live and previous tournaments.

To see more information about any of the listed tournaments you can click on their corresponding "details" button. This will include information such as the date, winner, seed (if any), matches, results, tournament and match bets, and a log for the entire tournament. New tournaments will also have access to the bracket and tournament odds information.

The details for a live tournament is more interactive. You can join the tournament as either a "competitor", or a "spectator". A competitor will be included in the bracket, while a spectator will not. Both can participate in betting though. Joining as either for the first time for any given tournament will reward you with 1000 coins.

Once an admin starts the tournament you can place tournament bets. The percentage chance of winning and their respective odds will be shown for each competitor in the bracket. You may place bets on a total of 3 different people with each individual bet being up to 500 coins. To cancel a bet, make a new bet on the same user equal to the negative sum of your existing bets on that person. A message that these bets have been cancelled should appear at the top, and you will receive your original coin back. Tournament bets will be closed once betting is closed on the first match by an admin.

Until the tournament is completed, at least one match will be active (i.e. available to complete next) for playing and betting. Match betting will be available for each given active match until it is closed by an admin. Results will then be updated by an admin, so keep an eye out that they are doing their job correctly.

Match bets can be made on active matches before the betting is closed. Making bets works very similarly to tournament bets, but the rules are slightly different. For match bets, you can only bet on one person in a match and the bets can not sum up to more than 1000 coins. You also can not bet on any finals match if you have an active tournament bet. Match bets can also be cancelled by entering the negative sum of existing bets.

The betting rules have been made in honor of Brandur. They have been proven to work as even Brandur will testify to.

At any point during a live tournament you can "redeem" 3000 coins for a prize. Show the redeem message to your admin or prize giver to receive the prize.

Any user who loses twice in the one tournament will be eliminated. The last user standing will be decalred the tournament winner.

Tournament bets will be paid out once all matches have been completed. Match bets will be paid out when the results are entered for that match.

Once the tournament is complete, an admin will close the tournament and it can then be viewed along with the other previous tournaments.

For further questions or feedback please contact me at danielkeith93@gmail.com

Have fun!

- Daniel

Admin Information

Congratulations on becoming an admin of Superposition Smash!

It will be outlined here what special abilities you have as an admin, but remember that with great power comes great responsibility. This system will only function and be enjoyable if this role is carried out with other's best interests in mind. Make sure to let others know you are an admin so that you can help them if needed.

As an admin you have two main roles. One is to manage accounts, and another for creating and running tournaments. Firstly, you can access the "manage accounts" page from your "account stats" page. On the "manage accounts" page you can:

  • Add a new account to the database. Make sure the account name is unique and correct as it cannot be changed easily (without a full deployment of the web app) afterwards.
  • You can deposit additional funds to the bank. Keep an eye on this value and try to make sure it does not go below zero. Going below zero shouldn't be the end of the world if it occurs, but may lead to unintended consequences (read: bugs).
  • Delete any account. This can not be undone.
  • Delete any tournament. This can not be undone. Make sure to copy the tournament log on the "tournament details" page before deleting if you intend to save the information.
  • Update any attributes of the account information. This is a hard overwrite and should only be used as a last resort. It may even be simpler to re-deploy. Entries must be of the correct format for a successful update though. Enter all the information you require to overwrite in the appropriate fields and then click and confirm "update".
  • Update who is an admin. This is the main feature of the update account fields. Enter "True" or "False" into the "admin" field to change the admin status of an account. Make sure that there is always at least one admin active, and that new admins can be responsible.

Secondly you must help create and run tournaments by:

  • Creating a new tournament in the "tournaments" page. Make sure it has a unique name.
  • Starting the tournament once all competitors and spectators have entered by clicking "start tournament". The tournament can be re-started with a matching seed at any time, but the seed is optional.
  • Entering the match results as they are completed. Pay careful attention that results are not input correctly as they can not be fixed without restarting. Margin of victory (MOV) corresponds to the number of lives/games (up to 3) that the victor won by.
  • Close the betting for each individual match once the match begins to ensure users can not bet when already knowing the partial result. Betting can not be re-opened after closing.
  • Closing the tournament by clicking "close tournament" in the "join tournament" section. Once users go back out the tournament will then be viewable in the previoius tournaments list.

Thank you for helping out!
